What is Bs CpE?
A Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering is 4 year program that combines aspects of computer science and electrical engineering to prepare students for careers in designing, developing, and maintaining computer hardware and software systems
What we do?
We engage in a variety of activities, projects, and initiatives during their studies to gain practical experience, enhance their skills, and prepare for careers in the field of computer engineering.
